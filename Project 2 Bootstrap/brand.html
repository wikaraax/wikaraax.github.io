SS Project Special Collection

*METARMORPHOSIS MMXXI*

The meaning behind the brand “Shadow Star” resonates deeply about living and embracing your shadow, your own insecurity. The brand is a testimony of my life and of my own insecurities. The SS Project Special Collection symbolizes Indonesia's national symbol, the "Garuda". A great deal of symbolism runs through the Garuda. The eagle is a symbol of creative energy. Its principal color, gold, symbolizes the greatness of the nation.

SS Project Shadow Star

SS Project Hoodie Black V1

“The only one who can stop you is your own shadow”. The SS Projek Shadow Star product line is the main product line of the brand "SS Project". The constellation behind the logo represents the dots of life. Every point of success, struggle, milestone in life is shown as a particle. A single particle envisions a blur path, but combining together represents a bright star, your originality. The brand embodies a celestial reminder of “WHO YOU ARE”.
